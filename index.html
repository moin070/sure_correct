What Are Clear Aligners and How Do They Work?

SureCorrect aligners are transparent plastic trays that fit over your teeth to help align them over time across all age groups (10-50 yrs) for adults, teens and kids. Aligners are often used as an alternative to traditional braces.

Invisible aligners, also known as clear braces, are designed as per the 3D scan of your teeth to fit you perfectly.

Various sets of aligners are shared on the basis of your predictive smile plan to gradually move your teeth to the desired position, to be worn for 20-22 hours every day.

The duration of the treatment time may vary on the basis of the complexity of the misalignment of each case. The process can typically take anywhere from six months to two years.
